### What is DocuSeal?

DocuSeal is one of the most powerful and reliable open-source document signing solutions available. It provides a self-hosted platform that ensures your sensitive documents and data remain under your control, offering an industry-standard alternative to proprietary SaaS platforms.

Built with security and ease of use in mind, DocuSeal provides:

*   **Deduplication & Efficiency:** Streamlined document processing to minimize overhead.
*   **Mobile Optimization:** Review and sign documents from any device, anywhere.
*   **Cryptographic Security:** Ensures the integrity and authenticity of every signature.
*   **Incremental Progress:** Real-time tracking of who has signed and who is pending.
*   **Open Source Foundation:** Transparent, community-driven development that you can trust.

DocuSeal allows organizations to protect their critical documentation workflows without being locked into expensive, opaque third-party services.

### Key Features of DocuSeal Plus

#### 1. Advanced User Management & Roles
Take full control over your organization's document access. DocuSeal Plus introduces a granular permission system with multiple roles:
*   **Admin:** Full system control and configuration.
*   **Editor:** Create and manage templates and submissions.
*   **Viewer:** Review documents and status without modification rights.

#### 2. Company Logo Personalization (Branding)
Maintain a professional image with your clients. DocuSeal Plus allows you to replace default branding with your own **Company Logo and Name**. Your custom branding appears on the signature forms and completion pages, ensuring a consistent brand experience for your signers.

#### 3. Automated Email Reminders
Never let a document stall again. DocuSeal Plus adds a powerful **Email Reminder** system. You can configure automated schedules to gently remind signers about pending documents, significantly increasing completion rates and reducing manual follow-up time.
